15 Jun 2023

Annual Day Celebration

Boys Campus Auditorium 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Our school celebrated its 28th Annual Day with great enthusiasm and fanfare. The event was attended by students, parents, faculty members, and distinguished guests from the education sector.

Event Highlights
  • Welcome speech by Principal Mr. Ali Raza
  • Cultural performances representing all provinces of Pakistan
  • Drama performance on the theme "Education for All"
  • Traditional dances by students from different grades
  • Prize distribution ceremony for academic excellence
  • Special recognition for extracurricular achievements
Guest of Honor

The event was graced by the presence of Dr. Samina Qadir, Director of Rawalpindi Education Board, who praised the school's commitment to holistic education and student development.

Student Participation

Over 200 students participated in various performances, showcasing their talents in music, dance, drama, and public speaking. The event was coordinated by our cultural committee headed by Mrs. Farah Naz.

05 Jun 2023

Inter-School Science Fair Winners

3 Gold Medals 12 Participants

Our students made us proud by winning 3 gold medals at the prestigious Rawalpindi Inter-School Science Fair held at Punjab Science College. The competition featured participation from 35 schools across the region.

Winning Projects
  • Automatic Water Management System by Ahmed Khan (Grade 10) - Gold Medal
  • Solar-Powered Air Purifier by Fatima Ali (Grade 9) - Gold Medal
  • Smart Waste Segregation by team of 3 students (Grade 11) - Gold Medal
Judges' Comments

The judges praised our students for their innovative approach to solving real-world problems using scientific principles. They particularly appreciated the practical applicability of the projects.

Next Steps

The winning teams will now represent Rawalpindi at the National Science Exhibition to be held in Islamabad next month. Our science faculty is working with the students to further refine their projects.

25 May 2023

New Girls Campus Building Inaugurated

Girls Campus 12 New Classrooms

The new wing of our Girls Campus was officially inaugurated by the District Education Officer, Mrs. Naila Kausar, in a ceremony attended by faculty, students, and parents.

Facility Highlights
  • 12 modern, air-conditioned classrooms
  • 2 state-of-the-art science laboratories
  • Computer lab with 30 workstations
  • Expanded library with reading area
  • Art and music rooms
  • Indoor activity space
Inauguration Ceremony

The ribbon-cutting ceremony was followed by a tour of the new facilities. Students presented a cultural program to mark the occasion. The DEO praised the school's commitment to providing quality infrastructure for girls' education.

Future Plans

With this expansion, the Girls Campus can now accommodate 200 additional students. Admissions for the new session will begin next week.

18 May 2023

Annual Book Fair

18-20 May 15 Publishers

Our school hosted a successful three-day book fair with participation from leading educational publishers across Pakistan. The event saw enthusiastic participation from students, parents, and teachers.

Event Features
  • Special discounts (20-30%) on all books
  • Meet-the-author sessions with renowned writers
  • Reading corners with comfortable seating
  • Book donation drive for underprivileged schools
  • Workshops on developing reading habits
Participating Publishers

The fair featured stalls from Oxford University Press, Paramount, Ferozsons, AFAQ, and other leading publishers covering textbooks, reference books, and general reading material.

Student Involvement

Our student library committee managed a second-hand book exchange stall that was particularly popular. The fair concluded with a book reading competition with prizes for winners.

10 May 2023

District Sports Champions

1st Position Basketball

Our school's basketball team created history by winning the Rawalpindi District Championship for the third consecutive year, defeating defending champions in a thrilling final match.

Tournament Highlights
  • Team won all 5 matches in the tournament
  • Final score: 68-62 in our favor
  • Captain Usman Khan named MVP of the tournament
  • Coach Mr. Tariq Javed awarded Best Coach
Key Players

The team's success was led by captain Usman Khan (Grade 12), point guard Ali Raza (Grade 11), and power forward Bilal Ahmed (Grade 10). Their teamwork and strategy were praised by all observers.

Next Challenge

The team will now represent Rawalpindi at the Punjab Inter-District Championship next month. Special training sessions have been arranged to prepare for the bigger challenge.

28 Apr 2023

Teacher Wins National Award

National Award Science Department

Mrs. Ayesha Khan, our senior science teacher with 15 years of experience, was honored with the prestigious National Excellence in Teaching Award at a ceremony in Islamabad.

Award Criteria

The award recognizes teachers who demonstrate exceptional commitment to student learning, innovative teaching methods, and contributions to educational development. Mrs. Khan was selected from over 500 nominees nationwide.

Teaching Innovations
  • Developed low-cost science experiments using everyday materials
  • Pioneered the "Science in Daily Life" project-based learning approach
  • Created YouTube channel with instructional videos for students
  • Mentored 5 teachers who went on to win district-level awards
School Celebration

The school organized a special assembly to honor Mrs. Khan's achievement. Students presented a tribute highlighting her impact on their learning journey. The principal announced a scholarship in her name for deserving science students.

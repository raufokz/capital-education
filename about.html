Our Story

Providing quality education since 1995

Capital Education System was founded with a vision to create an institution that would provide quality education while nurturing Islamic values and character development. What began as a small school has now grown into one of Rawalpindi's most respected educational institutions with separate campuses for boys and girls.

Over the past two decades, we have consistently maintained high academic standards while focusing on holistic development of our students. Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields, both nationally and internationally.

Our Core Values

Guiding principles that shape our educational approach

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops students intellectually, spiritually, and socially, preparing them to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world while maintaining strong moral values.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for academic excellence, character building, and producing responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, Compassion, and Lifelong Learning form the foundation of our educational philosophy and daily practices.

Our Journey

Milestones in our educational excellence

1995

Founded as a small primary school with just 50 students and 5 teachers in a modest building.

2002

Expanded to secondary level and established separate campuses for boys and girls.

2008

Introduced computer labs and modern teaching methodologies across all grades.

2014

Celebrated our first Board Examination top position holder in Rawalpindi district.

2020

Implemented smart classrooms and digital learning platforms across both campuses.

2023

Celebrated 25 years of educational excellence with over 1500 students and 100+ staff members.

Welcome to Our Boys Campus

A nurturing environment for academic excellence and character development

Established in 1995, our Boys Campus provides quality education from Kindergarten to Matriculation. We focus on creating well-rounded individuals through a balanced approach to academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.

Our campus features modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a safe learning environment tailored specifically for boys' educational needs and development.

Classes from KG to 10th Grade
Science and Computer Labs
Sports and Physical Education
Islamic Education and Character Building

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure for optimal learning

Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with smart boards and comfortable furniture to enhance the learning experience.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped physics, chemistry, and biology labs for practical learning and experimentation.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with latest technology and internet access for digital literacy and IT education.

Library

Well-stocked library with a wide range of books, reference materials, and reading area to encourage reading habits.

Sports Facilities

Indoor and outdoor sports facilities including cricket, football, basketball, badminton, and table tennis.

Prayer Area

Clean and spacious prayer area for daily prayers and Islamic education activities.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive curriculum for holistic development

Primary Section (KG to Grade 5)

Our primary program focuses on foundational skills in:

  • English, Urdu, and basic Arabic
  • Mathematics and general science
  • Islamic studies and moral education
  • Art, music, and physical education
  • Basic computer skills

We emphasize activity-based learning to make education engaging and effective.

Secondary Section (Grade 6 to 10)

Our secondary program prepares students for board examinations and beyond:

  • Science and computer studies
  • Mathematics and English advanced courses
  • Pakistan studies and Islamic education
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Debates and public speaking

Special focus on critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

About Us

Capital Education System is a premier educational institution in Qulzar e Quaid, Rawalpindi, providing quality education since 1995 through separate campuses for boys and girls.
